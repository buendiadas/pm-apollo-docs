Gnosis Apollo is a collection of packages that allows anyone to roll your own *Gnosis Prediction Market Interface* or *Gnosis Olympia*.

It consists of the `pm-contracts`, `pm-js`, `pm-trading-db` and `pm-trading-ui`.
